Workflow: Turning raw research notes into structured internal knowledge-base answers.
User: Graduate Research Assistants and primary investigators.
Input: Rough, unformatted bullet points or rough notes taken during literature reviews or meetings.
Output: A clean, standardized summary with a "Key Takeaways" section, "Methodology" summary, and "Action Items."
Value: It saves researchers hours of manual formatting, ensures all internal documentation is consistent, and makes past research easily searchable for the whole team.
